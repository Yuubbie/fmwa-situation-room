Public Reporting Channel

Report an Issue or Concern

If you have information about a child protection concern, a gender-based violence case, a humanitarian situation, or anything else relevant to the Ministry's work, you can report it here. A staff member reviews every submission before any action is taken; nothing is published automatically.

Leave blank to report anonymously. This is never shown publicly either way.

Privacy: Reports are only visible to authorised Ministry staff for review. They are never published publicly, and your contact information (if provided) is used only if a staff member needs to follow up with you directly.
